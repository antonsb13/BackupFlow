# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 14, 2026

## Overview
BackupFlow is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe that your data belongs to you. This document outlines how BackupFlow handles your information.

## Data Collection and Storage
BackupFlow is a local-first application. **We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any remote servers.**

All application settings, synchronization history, and file paths are stored exclusively in your local macOS `UserDefaults` and local application support directories.

## File Access
BackupFlow utilizes standard macOS security-scoped bookmarks to access the folders and drives you explicitly authorize for synchronization. This access is strictly limited to the necessary reading and writing required to perform the `rsync` mirroring operation. We do not analyze or parse the contents of your files.

## Telemetry
BackupFlow does not utilize analytics, crash reporters, tracking pixels, or any telemetry of any kind. 

## Contact
If you have questions regarding this privacy policy, please review the local source code available in the repository.
